## Key Architecture Decisions
- IPC: Flask on localhost (not stdin/stdout) — cleaner async progress reporting
- Bundling: conda-pack (not PyInstaller) — TF compatibility, avoids hidden-import hell
- Model: Downloaded at build time from GCS (not in repo — 1.5GB)
- Keep-alive sidecar: Spawned on app launch, persists full lifetime (model load 10-30s)
- TF version: Pin to 2.9.x — newer versions break Magenta's compat layer
